SpireSQL has been deployed!

1. Get the PostgreSQL connection:
   kubectl get svc {{ include "spiresql.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

2. Connect with psql:
   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "spiresql.fullname" . }} 5432:5432 -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
   psql -h localhost -p 5432

3. Monitor pods:
   kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "spiresql.name" . }}" -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

SpireSQL connects to SpireDB at: {{ .Values.spiredb.service }}:{{ .Values.spiredb.port }}
